Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Overview

Workplace Fairness ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Account Information

When you register, we collect your email address and an encrypted password. This is used solely for authentication.

User-Generated Content

We store data you create within the App, including complaints, compliance checklists, and chat messages. This data is associated with your account so you can access it across sessions.

Usage Data

We may collect basic usage data such as pages visited and features used to improve the App. We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the App's features
  • To authenticate your account
  • To process your complaints and compliance tracking
  • To provide AI-powered chat responses (chat messages are sent to our AI provider for processing)
  • To process subscription payments through Stripe

Third-Party Services

Stripe: We use Stripe to process payments. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data. We do not store your credit card information.

AI Chat: Chat messages are processed by Anthropic's Claude AI. Messages are sent to their API for response generation. Refer to Anthropic's privacy policy for how they handle API data.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We use HTTPS for all data transmission.

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your data through the App's dashboard
  • Deletion: Contact us to request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Contact us to request a copy of your data

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us through the App.
